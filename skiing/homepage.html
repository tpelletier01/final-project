Imagine the Rocky Mountains with an air of laid-back California cool, and you begin to understand Lake Tahoe. The water is so deep you could sink the Empire State Building and still not see the top. But it's the mountains surrounding it that make Tahoe a skier's paradise, with legendary resorts--Kirkwood! Heavenly! Northstar!--on peaks poking through trees and clouds at over 10,000 feet.Although Tahoe's ski areas are all within easy reach of one another, each of my favorites has a distinct look, personality and core audience.
