Straddling California and Nevada, Heavenly spreads across 4,800 acres of mostly intermediate terrain, with lake views on one side and desert on the other. The resort has over 34 miles of groomed cruisers, with all but two of the 30 lifts serving blue runs on 3,500 feet of vertical. The location on Tahoe's south shore gets 300+ days of sunshine a year yet also manages 360 inches of snowfall annually. Unlike Kirkwood, Heavenly delivers on the non-skiing options of a ski vacation: hot chocolate, ice skating, breweries, sleigh rides, shops, restaurants. And casinos! On the Nevada side, you can practically snowboard to the blackjack table, if that's your thing. The gondola takes you from Heavenly village to Adventure Peak, where beginners, intermediates and experts can find their own way. Oh, and there's a tubing hill there, too, and a ropes course and roller coaster in warmer months.
