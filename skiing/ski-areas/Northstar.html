Lake sophistication is the general vibe at Northstar California on Tahoe's stunning north shore. For those up for the splurge, the Northstar experience goes all the way to platinum: There's a platinum first-tracks experience that lets you ski before slopes open to the public, a platinum lift pass that frees you of pesky lines, and a platinum toast at an outdoor mountain bar with private seating and a demi-bottle of Veuve Clicquot. Such luxe surroundings puts Northstar on par with resorts like Deer Valley, Beaver Creek and Aspen.

Even without the bubbly, the Northstar experience is plenty posh. There are over 3,100 skiable acres with 20 lifts and two gondolas, and world-class terrain parks, including one with a 22-foot superpipe designed by Shaun White. Though mostly intermediate, expert skiers quietly head to the Backside for double-black joyrides through the trees. Kids adore The Stash, a terrain adventure park with bumps and jumps and just enough slopestyle magic to make them feel like li'l shredders.
